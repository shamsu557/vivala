About Us

Welcome to Vivala Park! Located at Katsina Road Kofar Ruwa, Kano, opposite Bocavu Barrack, we offer a unique blend of relaxation and adventure. From serene landscapes to thrilling attractions, Vivala Park is the perfect destination for families, friends, and individuals seeking unforgettable experiences. Our mission is to create cherished memories in a space that celebrates nature, community, and fun. Come and explore the magic of Vivala Park today!
